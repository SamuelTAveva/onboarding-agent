## Day 2 – Day 5

> Items marked **(EM)** are owned by your Engineering Manager. Items marked **(Mentor)** are led by your assigned mentor. Your action items are unmarked.

### Administrative
- [ ] Work through New Hire Checklist in Workday
- [ ] Benefits paperwork — handled by HRServices virtually (I-9 verification, CFIUS documentation)
- [ ] Add picture to Teams and Workday
- [ ] Set up Outlook and Teams on your phone

### Access & Licenses
- [ ] Confirm ADO access with your EM **(EM)**
- [ ] Verify Architecture wiki access: [https://dev.azure.com/AVEVA-VSTS/Architecture/_wiki/wikis/Architecture.wiki](https://dev.azure.com/AVEVA-VSTS/Architecture/_wiki/wikis/Architecture.wiki)
  - If you don't have access, check your VS subscription at [https://my.visualstudio.com/Subscriptions](https://my.visualstudio.com/Subscriptions?mkt=en-us)
  - Check user access at [https://dev.azure.com/AVEVA-VSTS/_settings/users](https://dev.azure.com/AVEVA-VSTS/_settings/users)
  - If your access is "Stakeholder" and your VS Subscription is not VSEnterprise, open a SupportBot work item requesting access to: Architecture wiki, osieng, and a VSEnterprise license. Provide mirror accounts of devs already on the team.
- [ ] Add your osisoft account as an alternate account at [https://my.visualstudio.com/Subscriptions](https://my.visualstudio.com/Subscriptions?mkt=en-us) to gain OsiEng access
- [ ] Request Pluralsight access via ServiceNow: [Generic access request ticket](https://aveva.service-now.com/sp/?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=09eb1b541b4030501b1dc88b274bcb0b&sysparm_category=null&catalog_id=-1&referrer=popular_items) **(EM)**
- [ ] Subscribe to Sphinx Shared Calendar **(Staff)**

### Training
- [ ] LinkedIn Learning setup **(EM)**
- [ ] Complete KnowBe4 security training **(EM to assign)**
